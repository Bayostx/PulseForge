Getting Started

PulseForge is a decentralized crowdfunding platform built on the Stacks blockchain. Follow these steps to get started:

1

Connect Your Wallet

Install Hiro Wallet or Xverse and connect it to PulseForge.

2

Explore Campaigns

Browse active campaigns and review their milestones and analytics.

3

Back or Create

Support existing campaigns or create your own with custom milestones.

Creating a Campaign

Launch your own milestone-based crowdfunding campaign in minutes.

Campaign Details

  • Title: Clear, descriptive campaign name (max 100 characters)
  • Description: Explain your project vision and goals (max 500 characters)
  • Token Type: Choose STX or approved SIP-010 tokens
  • Funding Goal: Set realistic target based on project needs
  • Duration: Campaign length (max 1 year)
(contract-call? .pulseforge create-stx-campaign
  "My Innovation Project"
  "Building the future of decentralized technology"
  u1000000 ;; Target: 1000 STX
  u1440) ;; Duration: ~10 days

Milestone Types

PulseForge supports multiple milestone verification methods:

1. Manual Milestones

Traditional voting-based approval by backers.

2. Time-Locked Milestones

Automatically verified when target block height is reached.

3. Funding Threshold Milestones

Triggered when campaign reaches specified funding level.

4. Conditional Milestones

Based on external verification data set by creator.

5. Multi-Dependency Milestones

Requires completion of prerequisite milestones.

Backing Campaigns

Support campaigns with milestone-based accountability.

How It Works

  1. 1 Choose a campaign and review its milestones
  2. 2 Contribute STX or approved tokens
  3. 3 Vote on milestone completion
  4. 4 Funds released as milestones are achieved
Protection: If a campaign fails to meet its goal or deliver milestones, backers can request refunds.

Smart Contract Reference

Key functions for interacting with PulseForge contracts.

Public Functions

;; Create STX campaign
(create-stx-campaign (title (string-ascii 100))
                  (description (string-ascii 500))
                  (target-amount uint)
                  (duration-blocks uint))
;; Back campaign with STX
(back-stx-campaign (campaign-id uint)
                  (amount uint))
;; Vote on milestone
(vote-milestone (campaign-id uint)
              (milestone-id uint))

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if a campaign doesn't reach its goal?

Backers can request full refunds if the campaign fails to meet its funding target by the end date.

How are funds released?

Funds are released incrementally as milestones are completed and approved by backers through voting.

Can I change milestones after campaign creation?

Milestones are locked once the campaign is created to ensure transparency and prevent manipulation.

What tokens are supported?

STX is always supported. SIP-010 fungible tokens can be used if approved by the platform.
